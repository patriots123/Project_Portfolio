About Me

My name is Will Shaughnessy and I’m a product manager turned web app developer in Boston, Massachusetts.

I am a graduate from the Thinkful full stack bootcamp and always eager to take on my next coding challenge. What I love most about coding is seeing my ideas come to life on the screen and the satisfaction that comes with solving complex problems and finally seeing the result of much effort and focus. I pride myself on the forethought that I put in before tackling a problem and always trying to implement solutions while upholding the best industry and coding standards I can. Besides coding, I love being out on the golf course, trying new restaurants and breweries, and supporting all Boston sports. Checkout my different projects on this site as well as in my GitHub profile and feel free to reach out especially if you want to talk tech.
